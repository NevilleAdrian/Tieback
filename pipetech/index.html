About us

Tieback Energy Engineering is a dynamic, energy services provider to the oil and gas industry with a global outlook. Tieback is a duly registered and wholly owned Nigerian Corporation fully embracing the African environment and business ethos.

We provide outstanding services in Engineering, Procurement, Construction Management (EPCM), Operations and Maintenance as well as Sub-surface solutions to address operational challenges facing the oil and gas industries.
